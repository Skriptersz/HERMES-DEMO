# Enable Web-to-Lead in Salesforce (30 seconds)

## Steps:

1. In Salesforce Setup, Quick Find → type **"Web-to-Lead"**
2. Click **Web-to-Lead** (under Feature Settings → Marketing)
3. Click **Edit** button
4. Check the box: **☑ Web-to-Lead Enabled**
5. Click **Save**
